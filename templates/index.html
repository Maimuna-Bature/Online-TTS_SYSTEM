About MyTTS

MyTTS lets you convert text or documents (.txt, .docx, .pdf) to speech using natural voices.
1. Type or upload your file.
2. Choose a voice.
3. Click Convert and download or listen instantly.
Your recent conversions are saved in the History tab.
